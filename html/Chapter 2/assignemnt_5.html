Rajesh Hamal (Nepali boen 9 June 1964) is a Nepalese film actor and televisoin presenter. He is widely
Considered as the greatest actor in Nepali Cinema and is often referred to by the fans and media as "Maha Nayak"(Nepali for "The great Actor").
Hamal began his acting career in 1991, peaking in the 1990s and early 2000s. He has set several box office records in Nepali Cinema.
